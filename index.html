About Tayari Rides

Empowering Ugandan Riders Since 2022

Tayari Rides was started to solve one problem: young Ugandans have the hustle but lack access to a motorcycle. We provide brand new bodas on affordable loan so you can start earning from day one.

We’ve helped over 1200 riders across Kampala, Wakiso, and Mukono become their own bosses. With flexible repayments, rider training, and mechanical support, we’re more than a loan company — we’re your partner on the road.

To empower boda riders with accessible motorcycle financing, training, and support so they can build sustainable livelihoods and financial independence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about owning a boda with Tayari Rides

What do I need to qualify for a motorcycle loan?

Just 3 things: 1. Valid National ID / NIN 2. One guarantor with NIN 3. 15% - 30% down payment depending on the plan. No land title or bank statement needed. Approval takes 24-48 hours.

How much do I pay daily and for how long?

It depends on the plan. Starter: From UGX 9,500/day for 24 months. Standard: From UGX 12,000/day for 18 months. Fast Track: From UGX 16,500/day for 12 months. You pay via Mobile Money daily, weekly, or monthly.

Who owns the motorcycle during the loan period?

Tayari Rides owns the motorcycle until you finish all payments. Once you complete your plan, the bike is transferred to your name 100% with no extra charges.

What happens if I miss a payment or the bike breaks down?

Talk to us early and we’ll reschedule. For breakdowns, we offer 24/7 roadside assistance and 6 months free servicing on Standard and Fast Track plans. GPS tracking is installed for security.

Do you provide training and insurance?

Yes. We provide free rider training, help with PSV license, and comprehensive insurance is included. We also help you connect to a good stage to start earning immediately.
